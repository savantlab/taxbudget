Cookie Usage
What cookies do we use?

We use only one cookie:

  • tax_allocator_user_id - A random identifier (UUID) to track your submission history
What information is collected?
  • A random anonymous identifier (no personal information)
  • Your tax allocation percentages when you submit
  • Timestamp of your submissions
What is NOT collected?
  • No personal information (name, email, address)
  • No tracking across other websites
  • No advertising or analytics cookies
  • No location data
Why do we use this cookie?

The cookie allows you to:

  • View your submission history across visits
  • Track how your allocations change over time
  • Compare your current and past allocations
Can I delete my data?

Yes! You can:

  • Clear your browser cookies to reset your identifier
  • Decline cookies - the app will work without saving history
  • Contact us to request data deletion
Privacy First: This is a minimal, privacy-respecting implementation. We don't sell data, we don't track you across the web, and we don't use third-party analytics.
